Patrick Hidalgo is a financial industry executive with over twenty years of buy side capital markets experience. Most recently, he spent nearly ten years working for a Denver based mortgage backed securities hedge funds as the Director of Trading Operations. Prior to that, he spent six years monetizing $15 billion home equity lines of credit, fixed rate seconds and subprime loans for Countrywide. He has an MBA from the University of Illinois and undergraduate degree in finance from the University of Colorado. Patrick is currently a volunteer for the Cleantech Open and Rockies Venture Club.
